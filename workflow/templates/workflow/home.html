DDaT Approval Workflow

The Approval Workflow service enables you to select an approval workflow and follow a process to complete a predefined task. The service provides a graphical representation of the workflow and its current status.

Building your own workflow

Tasks are easily defined and customised. You can either extend this service or build your own service and quickly leverage the capabilities of the Workflow executor. There are some built-in views and task types and you can create your own if required.
